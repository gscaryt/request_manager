Settings — your working library

This is your layer on top of the active library — it is never locked. Tick preselected to start a request selected on every reset. Tag EA · Editor to choose which checklist tabs show it. Click a request’s name to adapt its title and text for your field (system text can always be restored); use × to hide it. Your own one-off additions are custom requests. The shared foundation itself is changed in Setup (Director role).
